matplotlib.bezier.BezierSegment.polynomial_coefficients (   self) 
    The polynomial coefficients of the Bezier curve.

    .. warning:: Follows opposite convention from `numpy.polyval`.

    Returns
    -------
    (n+1, d) array
        Coefficients after expanding in polynomial basis, where :math:`n`
        is the degree of the bezier curve and :math:`d` its dimension.
        These are the numbers (:math:`C_j`) such that the curve can be
        written :math:`\sum_{j=0}^n C_j t^j`.

    Notes
    -----
    The coefficients are calculated as

    .. math::

        {n \choose j} \sum_{i=0}^j (-1)^{i+j} {j \choose i} P_i

    where :math:`P_i` are the control points of the curve.

263 """

265 # matplotlib uses n <= 4. overflow plausible starting around n = 15.
266 if n > 10:
