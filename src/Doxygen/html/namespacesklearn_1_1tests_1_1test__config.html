sklearn.tests.test_config.test_config_threadsafe_joblib (   backend) 
Test that the global config is threadsafe with all joblib backends.
Two jobs are spawned and sets assume_finite to two different values.
When the job with a duration 0.1s completes, the assume_finite value
should be the same as the value passed to the function. In other words,
it is not influenced by the other job setting assume_finite to True.
116def test_config_threadsafe_joblib(backend):
117 """Test that the global config is threadsafe with all joblib backends.
118 Two jobs are spawned and sets assume_finite to two different values.
119 When the job with a duration 0.1s completes, the assume_finite value
120 should be the same as the value passed to the function. In other words,
